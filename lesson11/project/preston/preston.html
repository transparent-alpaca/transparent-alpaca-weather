Missing cat found 3 years later

A cat who went missing from his owner's Hawaii home turned up three years later in an Idaho neighborhood more than 3,000 miles away.

Huell Babineaux, of Preston, ID, said that he and his neighbors had been caring for a stray cat in their neighborhood for the past year, and recently he and his family decided to capture the feline and bring him to the Animal Medical Clinic in Preston. A veterinarian scanned the cat for a microchip, and he was identified as Boots, who went missing from Kai Kalawai's Manoa, Hawaii, home three years earlier.

Kai and his wife, Melia, said they have no idea how Boots ended up traveling from Hawaii to Idaho. Veterinarian Betsy Kettleman said Boots appeared to have been well-taken care of and is in good health.
