Fun Facts about the Salt Lake Temple:

- Announced: July 28th, 1847.
- Groundbreaking: February 14th, 1853.
- Dedicated: April 6th, 1893.
- Currently under an extensive renovation. It is expected to reopen in the year 2023.
- The Temple Square receives between 3 and 5 million visitors every year.
- The location of the temple determined the grid system of roads in Utah, still used to this day.

In our website, you will find information about our locations, services, amenities, and the Temples we provide accommodations for. We cater temple workers, patrons, families, members and friends who attend the Temple to serve, or to participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.
