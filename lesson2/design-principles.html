Design Principles | David Rios-Lazo

PARC: Alignment

Etsy

This website is neatly organized and aligned, each category is separated from each other and aligned in columns and rows.

PARC: Repetition

Epson

This website is organized in a repeating pattern: they show an image of the product or category, the title of the product or category, and a link to the webpage.

White Space and Clean Design

Indiana University

This website has a lot of white space, and the text fields and data they show is separated from each other with a lot of space between them.
