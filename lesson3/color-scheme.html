Color Scheme:

The color scheme for the website can be found at
https://coolors.co/e8eef0-edae49-00798c-003d5b-aad8d9-2f4a57
An interactive preview with the HEX codes is available below:


The Text in the Logo and Site Header will have three colors:
Sunray, Indigo Dye, and Metallic Seaweed

The Primary and Footer navigation text will be colored Powder Blue,
and these elements will have a Charcoal background.

The Headings (H1, H2, H3) will be Metallic Seaweed.

The Paragraphs (P) will be colored Charcoal.

The main Background color will be Alice Blue.
